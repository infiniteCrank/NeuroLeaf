Step 1: You start typing a greeting.
Step 2: The AutoComplete ELM predicts the most likely full greeting.
Step 3: That predicted greeting is encoded into a feature vector using the Encoder ELM.
Step 4: The Language Classifier ELM uses the vector to predict your language.
Step 5: The prediction is visualized with a confidence bar.

This all runs 100% in your browser. No backend. No latency. Pure neural joy.
